Our Motivations

Fonts make words more expressive,
both aesthetically and logically.
That's why we think it's important to detect them.

Our approaches

we use machine learning methods, including random forest, logistic regression, and convolutional neural network (CNN), to distinguish 13 fonts. Model parameters are optimized, so are the data pre-processed with principal component analysis (PCA).

Results + Discussion

Both random forest and CNN showed promising results, with high accuracies. The optimization of parameters is shown to be effective.

Conclusion + Outlook

We hope this project, achieved with simple models and low computation resources, can inspire people (including us) to further develop font detection to benifit industries and global communities.
